Call enable your Odoo!

A simple phone to make and receive calls in Odoo!

Features

  • Works with any compatible SIP provider (JsSIP lib inside).
  • 1 SIP line.
  • 1 simultaneous call.
  • SIP login is setup in user's preferences.
  • Answer-Mode: Auto SIP header support to automatically accept incoming calls.
